June 2025 Key Insights

Major Highlights

  • Massive CEC Recovery: Canadian Experience Class exploded from 500 to 6,000 ITAs (+1,100%), indicating major backlog clearing or policy shift.
  • Program-Based Surge: Total program-based draws increased by 583%, the highest growth rate in 2025 to date.
  • Education Pause: Education category saw complete suspension (1,000 → 0 ITAs), suggesting quota fulfillment or strategic reallocation.

  • PNP Growth: Provincial nominees increased by 77.1%, indicating strong provincial demand and federal support.
