üìÖ July 17, 2025 High Impact üá®üá¶ Federal

Express Entry General Draw #267 - 4,750 ITAs Issued

Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) conducted a significant Express Entry draw, issuing 4,750 Invitations to Apply (ITAs) with a minimum Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score of 491.

Draw Details:
  • ITAs Issued: 4,750
  • Minimum CRS Score: 491 points
  • Draw Date: July 17, 2025
  • Tie-breaking Rule: June 8, 2025 at 14:32:15 UTC

Strategic Impact: This large draw indicates continued high demand for skilled immigrants and maintains the recent trend of sub-500 CRS scores, providing opportunities for well-prepared candidates.

Ontario PNP Expands In-Demand Skills Stream Eligible Occupations

Professional Insight: This expansion reflects Ontario's targeted approach to addressing specific labor market needs, particularly in sectors experiencing acute shortages.

Enhanced Processing Standards for Study Permit Renewals

üìÖ July 18, 2025 High Impact ‚öñÔ∏è Federal Court

Federal Court Rules on Study Permit Renewal Documentation Requirements

In Chen v. Canada (IRCC), the Federal Court ruled that students have the right to submit additional documentation during study permit renewal reviews, even after initial submission.

Legal Precedent: This ruling establishes procedural fairness requirements for study permit renewals and may impact future application processing protocols.
