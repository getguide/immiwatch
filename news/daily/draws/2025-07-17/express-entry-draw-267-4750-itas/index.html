Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) conducted Express Entry General Draw #267 on July 17, 2025, issuing 4,750 Invitations to Apply (ITAs) with a minimum Comprehensive Ranking System (CRS) score of 491. Draw Volume & Implications

The issuance of 4,750 ITAs represents a significant invitation volume, continuing IRCC's pattern of substantial general draws in 2025. This volume suggests confidence in processing capacity and commitment to meeting annual immigration targets.

CRS Score Trends

The minimum CRS score of 491 maintains the sub-500 trend observed in recent draws, indicating a competitive but accessible threshold for well-prepared candidates. This score level reflects balanced supply and demand dynamics in the Express Entry pool.

For Current Candidates: Those with CRS scores between 480-500 should prepare for potential invitations in upcoming draws. Consider language retesting or additional credentials to improve competitiveness.

For Immigration Practitioners: Advise clients that general draws continue to provide viable pathways for permanent residence, with preparation strategies focused on achieving 490+ CRS scores.
