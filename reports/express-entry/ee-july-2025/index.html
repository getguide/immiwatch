🏥 Healthcare Priority

Healthcare professionals received the largest allocation with 4,000 ITAs (54% of monthly total), reflecting Canada's continued focus on addressing critical healthcare worker shortages across all provinces.

Key Insights

🇨🇦 Canadian Experience Class

CEC draw on July 8th issued 3,000 ITAs with a CRS requirement of 518 points, maintaining the pattern of consistent CEC selections for candidates with Canadian work experience.

🍁 Provincial Nominee Program

Two PNP draws totaling 402 ITAs with high CRS requirements (780-788 points), demonstrating the competitive nature of provincial nominations.

Year-to-Date Context

July's performance brings the 2025 year-to-date total to 49,403 ITAs across multiple draws. The month's balanced approach between healthcare category-based draws and traditional program-based selections demonstrates Canada's strategic immigration management.

📈 August Outlook

Based on historical patterns, August is expected to continue the bi-weekly PNP cycle with potential healthcare and CEC draws. The government's balanced approach between targeted categories and traditional programs appears set to continue.
